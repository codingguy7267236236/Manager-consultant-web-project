Business Case

Non-Executive Directors (NEDs) be employed to monitor and scrutinise manager performance

Manager performance is a critical factor for the success of any organisation. It affects the quality of work, employee engagement, customer satisfaction, and financial results. However, manager performance is often not evaluated or managed effectively, leading to slow growth, poor performance, lost business opportunities and low staff morale. To address this issue, we propose to use non-executive directors (NEDs) to oversee all manager performance management activities and procedures. NEDs are independent board members who are not involved in the day-to-day operations of the organisation but will provide executives with progress updates on manager targets and objectives and ensure all manager performance management tools and procedures are implemented as necessary.
