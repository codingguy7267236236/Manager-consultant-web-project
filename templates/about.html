About

As an Organisational Development Manager, I am passionate about enhancing manager performance and facilitating organisational change at the middle manager level. I have supported executives in various industries, such as ICI, ENRON, SUMITOMO ELECTRIC, SEMBCORO UTILITIES, DFS Furniture, O2, Trinity House and CRONER. Many senior executives and business owners trust and value my advice.


My main area of expertise is manager performance, where I assist executives to improve or replace underperforming managers. I have a wealth of practical experience in leading and managing both line managers and middle managers. For the last 20 years, I have trained and coached first-time middle managers in manager performance management.


My business goal is to provide comprehensive training and development programs to managers of managers, specifically focusing on manager performance management. I also offer training and coaching services for non-executive directors, enabling them to effectively monitor and measure manager performance. By doing so, I help executives to achieve their business goals and objectives and create a culture of continuous improvement and accountability in the organisation.
